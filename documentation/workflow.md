# AutoLitRev User Workflow

The literature review process in AutoLitRev follows a structured 7-step workflow:

## Step 1: Input Research Question

The user provides their core research question(s). This is the foundation for the entire review process.

## Step 2: AI-Powered Query Generation

Based on the research questions, AutoLitRev uses GPT-4o to generate optimized search queries. Each query includes an explanation of which aspect of the research it targets.

## Step 3: Corpus Suggestion & Customization

The system suggests relevant academic databases (e.g., PubMed, ArXiv, CrossRef) based on the research context. Users can manually include or exclude specific sources.

## Step 4: Article Search & Retrieval

AutoLitRev executes the generated queries against the selected databases. It fetches metadata, abstracts, and attempts to retrieve full-text documents where accessible.

## Step 5: Article Screening

Retrieved articles are presented in an interface where users can browse, read abstracts, and select which works should be included in the final review.

## Step 6: Structured Review Generation

The system assembles the selected literature into a coherent review based on a chosen methodology (Systematic, Scoping, Narrative, etc.). It uses AI to identify themes, findings, and gaps.

## Step 7: Editing & Exporting

The user can refine the generated review in an integrated editor and export the final document in formats like .docx or .pdf.
